Frequently Asked Questions

What is FleetTrack?

FleetTrack is a fleet management solution that helps businesses track vehicles, optimize routes, and monitor driver behavior in real-time.

Can I switch between monthly and annual plans?

Yes, you can switch between monthly and annual plans at any time. Annual plans offer a 20% discount compared to monthly plans.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all our plans. You can explore all features before committing to a subscription.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, and American Express. For custom plans, we also support bank transfers.
